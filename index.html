Animal Friends of Virginia is a foster-based, northern Virginia non-profit group affiliated with the Animal Friends of Barbour County.

What makes our group special?

We are partnering with Animal Friends of Barbour County (AFOBC), a 501(c)(3) non-profit, no-kill shelter located in a rural area of West Virginia. They have so many awesome animals just waiting for their forever homes. We are helping to broaden the audience for these wonderful animals by bringing them to Northern Virginia.
